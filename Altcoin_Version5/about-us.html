About Us

We service active private investors and instituitions

AltcoinMarket was founded in 2017 by a team of entrepreneurs with experience from the financial, legal and the international start-up world. Altcoinmarket is a complete token sale service firm for companies and private investors. We are a legally registered entity in Zug, Switzerland, with representation today in London and New York.

All team members and advisors have extensive experience of token crowd sales and the crypto currency industry. Our intent is to build an improved service for private investors and companies who are involved in the cryptocurrency world.
